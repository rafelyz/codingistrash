About Us!

Our Story:
W Interiors is a Wayne family business that was
established in 2016 after recognising a gap in
the market for high-quality coloured fixtures.
With expertise in high-end renovations, our
founders initially set out to sell renovation
packages to help streamline the selection and
building process. After discovering a lack of local
access to high-quality architectural products,
particularly coloured tapware, they combined their
diverse retail, architecture, and property
development backgrounds to create a solution.

The vision was to empower homeowners and trades by
providing premium, coloured kitchen and bathroom
products at an inclusive price point â€” thus
conceptualising the start of W Interiors.


We shifted into the e-commerce sphere, scaling the business by
creating greater accessibility for customers worldwide.
We continuously aim to facilitate wider access to our products as we
understand the importance of a tactile experience before purchasing.
For that reason, we have set up two additional showrooms in the
United Kingdom and New Zealand, with more on the way.


Today, we are globally recognised for our signature finishes of brushed brass,
brushed copper, brushed nickel, brushed gunmetal, matte black, and white.


We pride ourselves on being a comprehensive supplier for any project needs
and are dedicated to creating innovative products of exemplary quality.
